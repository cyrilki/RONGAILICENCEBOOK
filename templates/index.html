Welcome to the License Management System

The License Management System is a comprehensive platform designed to streamline the management of business licenses within the County Government of Nakuru, Rongai Sub-County. This system eliminates the need for manual record-keeping, providing a digital solution for adding, searching, and managing licenses efficiently. Whether you're a business owner, administrator, or government official, this system ensures transparency, accuracy, and ease of access to license-related information.

How It Works:
- Add Licenses: Authorized users can add new licenses by entering business details such as the business name, ID, owner's phone number, activity, location, and registration date.
- Search Licenses: Users can search for existing licenses by business name or ID, making it easy to retrieve specific information.
- Admin Features: Administrators have additional privileges, such as adding users, viewing user performance, and deleting licenses or users when necessary.
- User-Friendly Interface: The system is designed with a clean and intuitive interface, ensuring a seamless experience for all users.

How to Use:
1. Log In: Use your credentials to log in to the system. If you're a new user, sign up and set a password.
2. Navigate: Use the navigation bar to access different features such as adding licenses, searching licenses, or managing users (admin only).
3. Perform Actions: Add new licenses, search for existing ones, or manage user accounts based on your role.
4. Log Out: Always log out after completing your tasks to ensure the security of your account.

This system is a testament to the County Government of Nakuru's commitment to leveraging technology for better governance and service delivery. By digitizing license management, we aim to enhance efficiency, reduce errors, and provide a more transparent process for all stakeholders.
